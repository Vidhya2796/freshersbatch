ICECREAM RECIPE

Hello guys!! In this session, I'll let you know how to make icecream at home.

INCREDIENTS

  • Milk
  • Heavy Cream
  • Vanilla
  • Sugar

STEPS

  1. Pour the milk, heavy cream, vanilla, and sugar to a blender
  2. Blend until smooth.
  3. Add any chunky extras and blend again.
  4. Pour the mixture into a freezer safe container.
  5. Freeze the mixture for 6 to 8 hours.
  6. Serve the ice cream with an ice cream scoop and freeze any leftovers.
